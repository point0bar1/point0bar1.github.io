Mitch Hill

a Ph.D. Candidate in Statistics at the University of California, Los Angeles.

I am a part of the Vision, Cognition, Learning, and Autonomy (VCLA) lab.

My research focuses on unsupervised modeling of complex high-dimensional signals. In particular, I am interested in learning and mapping energy functions of image data.
