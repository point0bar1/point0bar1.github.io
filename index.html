Welcome to the home page of

Mitch Hill

Assistant Professor of Statistics and Data Science at the University of Central Florida.

I am the director of the Data Mining Lab at UCF.

My research focuses on statistical modeling of complex high-dimensional signals. My recent work investigates learning and mapping energy functions of image data and using generative models to defend against adversarial attacks that target image classifiers.
