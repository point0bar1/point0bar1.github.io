Research

My current research has two major branches:

  1. learning energy functions of high-dimensional data
  2. mapping the macroscopic structures of high-dimensional energy functions

Learning Energy Functions of High-Dimensional Data

An energy function is the term $U(x)$ of a Gibbs density $p(x) = \frac{1}{Z} \exp\{ - U (x) \} $. The energy function defines a physical system over the state space of $x$ that determines the distribution of states. In my work, the states $x$ are images and the function $U(x)$ is a Convolutional Neural Network.
